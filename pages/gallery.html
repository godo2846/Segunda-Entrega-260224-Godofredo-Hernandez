Yoga Bricks

Yoga blocks and bricks are used to assist in yoga practices. Usually, yoga bricks are used for stability and bringing the floor closer, as well as helping you build your flexibility and ease into more challenging poses. Yoga blocks are used for sitting, supporting the hips in restorative yoga positions as well as reducing pressure on your muscles and joints, helping with your overall alignment. Yoga bricks are made of firm materials like cork or foam, and are smooth to ensure a comfortable grip.
